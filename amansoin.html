Global Warming

Global warming is the increase in Earth's average temperature due to greenhouse gases.

Causes

  • Burning of fossil fuels
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial pollution

Effects

  • Rising sea levels
  • Extreme weather
  • Melting glaciers

Solutions

  • Plant more trees
  • Use renewable energy
  • Save electricity
